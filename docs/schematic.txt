================================================================================
  Instruction Encoding  (32-bit, little-endian in memory)
================================================================================

  I-type  (immediate operand):
   31      24  23   20  19   16  15                               0
  ┌──────────┬────────┬────────┬─────────────────────────────────┐
  │  opcode  │   rd   │  rs1   │            imm16                 │
  └──────────┴────────┴────────┴─────────────────────────────────┘
   8 bits      4 bits   4 bits   16 bits

  R-type  (register-register):
   31      24  23   20  19   16  15   12  11                      0
  ┌──────────┬────────┬────────┬───────┬──────────────────────────┐
  │  opcode  │   rd   │  rs1   │  rs2  │         (unused)          │
  └──────────┴────────┴────────┴───────┴──────────────────────────┘
   8 bits      4 bits   4 bits   4 bits   12 bits


================================================================================
  Memory Map
================================================================================

  Address       Size      Description
  ──────────    ────────  ─────────────────────────────────────────
  0x0000        61 440 B  RAM  (general purpose; programs load here)
  0xEFFE        2 B       Stack top (SP initialised here, grows ↓)
  0xF000        1 W       MMIO: STDOUT_CHAR  (write char to terminal)
  0xF001        1 W       MMIO: STDIN        (read char from terminal)
  0xF002        1 W       MMIO: STDOUT_NUM   (write decimal number)
  0xF004        1 W       MMIO: TIMER_LO     (cycle counter low word)
  0xF006        1 W       MMIO: TIMER_HI     (cycle counter high word)
  0xF008–0xFFFF           Reserved
